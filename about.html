Our Story

Protecting digital privacy for millions worldwide since 2015

Our Mission

Making Online Privacy Accessible to Everyone

Founded in 2015, SecureVPN began with a simple belief: everyone deserves the right to privacy online. What started as a small project by a group of privacy advocates has grown into one of the world's most trusted VPN services.

Today, we protect over 50 million users across 60+ countries, providing military-grade encryption and a strict no-logs policy that ensures your data stays yours.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Privacy First

We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. Our strict no-logs policy ensures your data is never stored or shared.

Transparency
